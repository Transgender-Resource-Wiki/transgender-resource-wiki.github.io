## Mental Health Resources

[The Trevor Project]&#58; Free mental health support and suicide hotline for LGBTQ people.

### United States

[988 Lifeline]&#58; United States only crisis hotline. Phone call available 24/7; text and webchat also available.

[Trans Lifeline]&#58; United States & Canadian peer support hotline by trans people for trans people. 

### Canada

[Youth Line]&#58; LGBT hotline support service; phone call and webchat available.

[Trans Lifeline]&#58; Canadian & United States peer support hotline by trans people for trans people. 

## Location Specific Resources

[Trans Resources]&#58; Search for local resources by entering a country or state.

### United States

[Erin in the Morning's Informed Consent HRT Map]&#58; Map of informed consent clinics, most of which are in the United States.

[Trans Health Project]&#58; Guide for choosing health insurance as a transgender person.

[LGBTQ Healthcare Directory]&#58; Searchable directory of LGBTQ healthcare providers in USA & Canada.

[ID Change Help]&#58; Resources for changing identification information on USA legal documents.

[Trans Resistance Network]&#58; Mutual aid orginization helping transgender people facing violence and discrimination.

[Southern Equality]&#58; Find resources in the south.

### Canada

[LGBTQ Healthcare Directory]&#58; Searchable directory of LGBTQ healthcare providers in Canada & USA.

### Germany

[Trans DB]&#58; Searchable database of medical services in Germany.

## News & Blog Sites

[Erin in the Morning]&#58; An independent transgender journalist.

[Gender Dysphoria Bible]&#58; A wealth of information on gender dysphoria.

[Stained Glass Woman]&#58; Essays about being transgender.

## For Allies
[Gender Spectrum]&#58; Guide for cisgender allies to help understand gender.
